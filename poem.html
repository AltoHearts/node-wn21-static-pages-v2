A Short Poem

From outter world the bleaty boi roam,
among the stars he call home.
A mask upon the starlight falls,
distance bleating the space heard calls.
How sound travels in space you ask?
The space deer has a headset in his mask.
This poem deer friend, makes no logical sense
Im mostly just trying to make contence.

Smooth Brain,

No Thoughts.

Head Empty,

Only code.

Maria O'Sullivan - AltoHearts
